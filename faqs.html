FAQs

1. What services does Nest Knockers offer?

We offer a variety of cleaning services, including standard home cleaning, deep cleaning, move-in/move-out cleaning, and customized cleaning plans to suit your specific needs.

2. How can I book a cleaning service?

Booking a cleaning service is easy! Go to the "Bookings" section in our app, select the type of cleaning service you want, choose a date and time, and confirm your booking.

3. Can I reschedule or cancel a booking?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel your booking up to 24 hours in advance. Simply go to the "My Bookings" section of the app and choose the booking you want to change.

4. Are your cleaners background-checked?

All of our cleaners are thoroughly vetted and undergo background checks to ensure your safety and peace of mind.

5. What should I do to prepare for a cleaning?

We recommend picking up any personal items and clearing clutter from surfaces to ensure our cleaners can focus on deep cleaning the areas you need most.

6. What is included in a standard cleaning service?

A standard cleaning includes dusting, vacuuming, mopping floors, cleaning the kitchen, and wiping down surfaces in the bathroom. You can view the full checklist when booking your service.

7. Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?

No, our cleaners will bring all the necessary cleaning supplies. If you prefer a specific product, you can note it in your booking details.

8. How is payment handled?

Payment is securely processed through the app. After booking, you can pay using a credit or debit card. We also offer other payment methods for your convenience.

9. What if I’m not satisfied with the cleaning?

If you're not satisfied with our service, please contact us within 24 hours, and we will address your concerns or arrange a re-clean if needed.

10. How can I contact customer support?

You can reach customer support via the "Help & Support" section in the app or by emailing us at support@nestknockers.com. We're here to help with any questions or issues!
